Step by Step Guide to buying a home

Step 1

Meet with a buyers agent

Tip

Discuss what type of home you’re looking for, location, priorities, deal-breakers and price range and any other important features of a home to you

Step 2

Start searching!

Have Fun!

With the help of your agent, identify homes you’d like to see and tour them.

Note what you like and dislike about each house, so you know what to look for (or avoid) in the future

Step 3

Make an offer

Contract

Found your dream house? It's time to make an offer.

Your agent will help you make the best offer based on current market conditions, value of the house and other factors.

Once terms are agreed, the house is under contract.

Step 4

Do your due dilligence

Don't Skip!

During this period, your agent will work with you on property inspections, getting through the title process and will work with your lender to get you to the closing table as soon as possible

Check for contingency clauses that let you back out if issues arise.

Step 5

Be to the church on-time!

Hooray!

It’s the day you’ve been waiting for- the day you get the keys to your new home.

You’ll need to sign a lot of paperwork and pay any closing fees, down payments and property taxes before the transfer of ownership is complete
