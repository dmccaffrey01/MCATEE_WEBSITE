Step by Step Guide to buying a home

Step 1

Meet with a real estate professional about selling your current home

Selling?

You may need to meet with a separate agent who specializes in selling homes in your area.

You'll receive insights on the home's market value, the ideal listing time, and any necessary improvements

Step 2

Find out about selling your current home

Tip

Online calculators can give you a rough estimate, but realtors will want to know you’re pre-approved by a lender before beginning your house search

Step 3

Meet with a buyers agent

Tip

Discuss what type of home you’re looking for, location, priorities, deal-breakers and price range

Step 4

Start searching!

Have Fun!

With the help of your agent, identify homes you’d like to see and tour them.

Note what you like and dislike about each house, so you know what to look for (or avoid) in the future

Step 5

Make an offer

Negotiate

Found your dream house? It's time to make an offer.

Your agent will help with offers and negotiations.

Once terms are agreed, the house is under contract.

Step 6

Do your due dilligence

Don't Skip!

During this period, you'll have a home inspection and property appraisal.

Check for contingency clauses that let you back out if issues arise.

Step 7

File your mortgage application paperwork

Tip

You don’t have to have a 20% down payment to buy a house any more.

Desert Canyon Mortgage offers programs that involve putting 1% down

Step 8

Meet with a real estate professional about selling your current home

Hooray!

It’s the day you’ve been waiting for- the day you get the keys to your new home.

You’ll need to sign a lot of paperwork and pay any closing fees, down payments and property taxes before the transfer of ownership is complete
