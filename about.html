Jenny Mc Atee

Team Leader, GSI, GRI, SRS, AHWD

Jenny has over 20 years in real estate investing experience. She has been a licensed sales agent since 2018 and has earned her GRI and SRES designations from the National Association of Realtors (NAR).

Jenny is also a Real Estate Educators Association GSI (Gold Standard Instructor). Along with her real estate experience, Jenny has an extensive background in Information Technology, Systems Management/Development, and project management.

She earned her M.A. from the University of Maryland Baltimore County in Adult Learning Theory, Instructional Design, and Human Performance. Jenny and her team service Maryland, Virginia, and the District of Columbia.

Jenny loves to teach, coach, and share her knowledge and experience so that others may benefit as they start on and continue their journeys in the world of real estate. Jenny has led many non-profit groups to greater success by being on several Board of Directors.

In her spare time, Jenny loves to ride her bike, travel, and participate in cultural events in her hometown of DC. An avid sports junky, she has season tickets to University of Maryland Football and can be seen and heard cheering for her beloved Capitals!

A very proud Navy Mom to her son, Liam, she will always say, “GO NAVY! BEAT ARMY!” Whether you are interested in buying, selling, investing, or need a top-performing mentor/coach, contact Jenny today at jenny@mcateegroup.com or (240) 595-2680 to get started achieving your goals.
